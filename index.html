Bears

A bear is a type of mammal that belongs to the family Ursidae. There are eight species of bears found in the Northern Hemisphere, including the polar bear, giant panda, and six other species. Bears are characterized by their distinctive physical features, such as their shaggy fur, powerful build, and distinctive snout.
